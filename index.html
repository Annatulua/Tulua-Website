Who we are

Built different.
On purpose.

Tulua was founded in Thalwil in 2011 with a simple belief: that a health club should feel like a privilege, not a transaction. No crowds. No noise. No one asking if you want a protein shake.


What you get instead is space to move, think, and recover — with trainers who know your name, equipment chosen with care, and a community that genuinely values what you are building together.

What we offer

From personal training to longevity protocols, from recovery to guided group programs — Tulua brings together everything your body needs under one roof.

Two clubs

Thalwil & Zollikon

Each location has its own character. Both share the same commitment to quiet excellence.

Thalwil

Est. 2011 — The original

Where Tulua began. Tucked beside Lake Zurich, our Thalwil club is the quieter of the two — intimate, carefully curated, and deeply familiar to the members who have trained here for years.

Zollikon

Est. 2020 — Expanded together

Our Zollikon club brought Tulua into one of Zurich's most considered neighbourhoods. Brighter, more expansive — and just as deliberate in everything it offers.

Our philosophy

Less members.
More meaning.

We deliberately keep our membership small. Not because we cannot grow — but because growth for its own sake is not what Tulua is about.


Our trainers work exclusively with Tulua members. The equipment is selected, not accumulated. The space is designed to feel like yours — because in many ways, it is.
