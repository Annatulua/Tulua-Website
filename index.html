Why Tulua

Not a gym.
A private sanctuary.

Most fitness clubs are designed for volume. Tulua was built for the opposite — a space where your training receives the full attention it deserves, in an environment that matches the standard you hold in every other area of your life.

Founded in 2011, we were among the first clubs in Switzerland focused solely on personal fitness. Fifteen years later, we've never once advertised. Our members find us by reputation alone.
