Thalwil

Switzerland · Est. 2011

Thalwil is where Tulua began. Founded in 2011 by Misho Farouk on the shore of Lake Zurich, it is the original — a private health club built around a single conviction: that your health deserves the same attention you give everything else that matters."

Fully equipped for personal training, body composition analysis, cryotherapy, and physiotherapy. Everything here has been refined over fifteen years of quiet, uncompromising practice.

What to Expect

A different kind
of experience.

From the moment you walk in, every detail has been considered. Not to impress — but to remove every possible distraction from what you came to do.

i
Arrive to your own space
Sessions are booked privately. When you arrive, the club is yours. No waiting, no strangers, no noise.
ii
Your trainer knows your program
Every session picks up exactly where the last one ended. Continuity is built into how we work — never assumed.
iii
Recovery is part of the session
Physiotherapy, cryotherapy, and massage are integrated into your program — not afterthoughts you book separately.
iv
Flexibility without compromise
Early mornings, late evenings, Sundays on request — your schedule drives ours. Both locations available to all members.
v
Absolute discretion, structurally
Our members include executives and public figures. Confidentiality isn't a policy — it's built into how the club is designed and operated.
