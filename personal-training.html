The Process

From first session
to lasting results.

Every Tulua journey follows a clear, considered process. Results don't happen by accident.

01
We get to know you

A private consultation to understand your goals, history, lifestyle and what has worked before and what has not.

02
We design your program

A bespoke training plan built around your physiology and schedule. InBody analysis included from day one.

03
We train: in private

Every session is yours. The floor is scheduled so it never gets crowded. No distractions, no compromise. The people you occasionally share the space with are here with the same level of intent.

04
We evolve together

Programs are reviewed and refined continuously. Recovery, nutrition and sleep are part of the conversation.
