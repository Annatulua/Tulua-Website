What is Echo

A structured way
into Tulua's world.

ECHO is a program born from Tulua's fifteen years of private training expertise. It offers a calm, structured environment where guided training, small-group sessions and recovery come together under one roof.

ECHO members train at the same standard, in the same spaces. Just in a format designed for those who want Tulua's quality without a fully private membership. Our additional Tulua services are available at regular member pricing for all ECHO 100 Society members.

Availability & Schedule

The ECHO 100 Society is a limited offer within Tulua Health Club's offering, open to a maximum of 100 members. Entry begins with a trial training session to ensure alignment with our approach. Once accepted, memberships carry a minimum term of three months and roll into subsequent three-month cycles. Cancellations may be made before renewal or at the discretion of the club. Training is scheduled according to trainer availability. ECHO upholds Tulua's principles of privacy, discretion and measured, high-level care.
